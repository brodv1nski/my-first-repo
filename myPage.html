I have implemented encode and decode function. By the name, you can understand what these functions will do. The decode function turns your message into different sets of numbers, separated by a space. The sum of the digits in each set will be equal to the ASCII code of a specific letter from your message.The decode function acts in the opposite direction

Encode

  • Only english letters,numbers and special symbols
  • Message length must be up to 10 symbols long

Decode

  • To get result you must use encode function first
  • Just paste the encode result at decode input field
